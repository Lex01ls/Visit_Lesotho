On the header lies the Navigation Panel which contains Home, Explore, Map, Gallery and Profile.

1. Press "Home" Button to go Home panel which a video will play automatically, you can also pause and play the video

2. Press "Explore" Button to go to Explore Panel  which will show Places of and the Description with their prices,
 you can also leave a comment, and it will be displayed on the Home Panel

3. Press "Map" Button to go to the Map Panel where Places of interest location are marked

4. Press "Gallery" Button to go to the Gallery Panel where you can see albums of Places of interest,
 you can change the media by pressing "Previous" and "Next" Buttons
